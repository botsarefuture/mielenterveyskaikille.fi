Mental Health for All! - Protest on June 3, 2024

Be a part of something big! Join us at Senate Square on June 3, 2024, and demand better mental health services for all Finns. It will be a peaceful, alcohol-free event where people of all ages can raise awareness of the mental health crisis and advocate for change.

Where does my donation go?

Every donation goes directly toward organizing the protest. If there's money left over, it will be donated directly to mental health organizations. With your support, we can create real change and bring hope to those who need it.
Expenses include communication and social media marketing, sound systems, and other technical arrangements. Even a small amount helps us a lot, as we rely on volunteers.
