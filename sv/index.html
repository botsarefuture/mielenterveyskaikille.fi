Mental health services are in crisis!


Help is not received in time. This can be seen, for example, from the fact that annually there are approximately 70,000 suicide attempts and 1000 suicides.
Additionally, too many people are left to struggle alone in the midst of difficulties without sufficient help and support.

Let's fight together for mental health - against the mental health crisis!
